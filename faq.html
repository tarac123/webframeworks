Frequently Asked Questions

What is this app about?

This app helps users connect with groups, stay updated with notifications, and explore exciting features. Download now to get started

Where can I download the app?

Neighbours in Nature is available to download on the Apple App Store or Google Play.

How do I join a group?

Simply open the app, browse available groups, and click "Join" to become part of a group.

Can I create my own group?

Yes, you can create a group within the app. Simply go to the "Groups" section, click on "Create Group," and follow the instructions to set up your group.

What devices support the app?

The app is available for both iOS and Android devices. You can download it from the App Store or Google Play.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the sign-in page. Follow the instructions to reset your password via email.
